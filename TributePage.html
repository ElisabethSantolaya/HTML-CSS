Some information about Dr. Norman Boralug

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist and humanitarian who led initiatives to combat world hunger. He is often credited with saving millions of lives through his work on improving crop yields and developing disease-resistant wheat varieties.

His research and innovations in agriculture, known as the Green Revolution, helped increase food production and alleviate hunger in many developing countries. Dr. Borlaug's contributions to agricultural science and his dedication to addressing global food security have left a lasting impact.
